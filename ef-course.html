Curriculum

10-module deep dive

Each module includes video lessons, guided exercises, downloadable worksheets, and reflection prompts.

01Orientation: The Dialectical Life
45 min
Understanding DBT philosophy, the biosocial theory, and why "both/and" thinking is the foundation of lasting change. Includes your course roadmap and goal-setting exercise.
02Mindfulness: Core Awareness Skills
1 hr
The "what" and "how" skills of mindfulness. Observe, describe, participate. Non-judgmentally, one-mindfully, effectively. Includes 5 guided mindfulness practices.
03Wise Mind & Mindful Decision-Making
50 min
Finding the synthesis of emotion mind and reasonable mind. Practical exercises for accessing wise mind in everyday decisions, relationships, and crises.
04Distress Tolerance: Crisis Survival
1.5 hrs
STOP, TIPP, self-soothing with five senses, IMPROVE the moment, pros and cons, and distraction with ACCEPTS. Build your personal crisis survival kit.
05Distress Tolerance: Reality Acceptance
1 hr
Radical acceptance, turning the mind, willingness vs. willfulness, and half-smiling/willing hands. Deep practice for accepting painful realities without adding suffering.
06Emotion Regulation: Understanding Your Emotions
1 hr
The function of emotions, the model for describing emotions, checking the facts, and understanding vulnerability factors. Includes emotion tracking worksheet.
07Emotion Regulation: Changing Your Responses
1.5 hrs
Opposite action, problem solving, ABC PLEASE skills for reducing vulnerability, and building mastery. Creating a life worth living through valued action.
08Interpersonal Effectiveness
1.5 hrs
DEAR MAN for getting what you need, GIVE for maintaining relationships, FAST for maintaining self-respect. Role-play exercises and real-world practice scripts.
09Hypnotherapy Integration
1 hr
How clinical hypnotherapy deepens DBT skill acquisition. Guided hypnotherapy recordings for emotional regulation, anxiety reduction, and rewiring automatic responses.
10Building Your Daily Practice
45 min
Creating sustainable routines that integrate all four modules. Your personalized skills plan, maintenance strategies, and the path from surviving to thriving.
