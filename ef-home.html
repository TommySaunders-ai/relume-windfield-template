About

Edward Flynn, LPC, NBCCH, DBTC

With over 13 years of clinical experience, Edward Flynn specializes in helping individuals build lasting resilience through evidence-based approaches. His integrative practice combines Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Clinical Hypnotherapy, and Mindfulness-Based techniques.

Edward's work spans private practice, intensive outpatient programs, and digital wellness resources, making clinical-grade tools accessible to those who need them most.
