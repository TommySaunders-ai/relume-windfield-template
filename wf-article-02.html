There is a moment in every commercial real estate transaction where the listing sheet stops being sufficient. A buyer asks about tenant buildout costs. An investor wants cap rate comparisons against recent submarket sales. A developer needs to know the zoning allows their intended use. The listing sheet says the building is 48,000 square feet of industrial space priced at $3.2 million. That is the beginning of a conversation, not the end of one. The Enrichment Agent exists to complete that conversation before it starts.

Enrichment AgentCONF 0.97

At Windfield Real Estate, every property that enters the pipeline goes through the same transformation. A raw Buildout listing containing 40–60 data points is processed by 12 specialist agents that produce a complete investment-grade profile containing approximately 340 data points. The raw listing tells a buyer what the building is. The enriched profile tells them why they should buy it.

03Marketing Copy: From Generic to Buyer-Specific

The Marketing Copy Specialist writes five descriptions — each calibrated to a different buyer persona. An owner-occupier receives copy about operational efficiency and buildout flexibility. An investor receives cap rate and value-add positioning. A developer receives zoning and density analysis. The marketing copy is the last enrichment step, not the first — because it synthesizes what the other 11 specialists discovered.

“A raw listing says the building is 48,000 square feet. An enriched profile says it has a 7.2% cap rate that is 80 basis points above the submarket average and accommodates automated racking for 3PL tenants.”

04Investment Thesis Generation

The Investment Thesis Specialist produces the single most valuable enrichment output. It takes raw financial data and produces a complete investment case: current cap rate versus submarket average, projected IRR under three scenarios, value-add opportunities with estimated ROI, and market timing analysis. The thesis references specific comparable transactions from S05 and specific market conditions from the Market Analysis Agent. This specificity is what converts investor inquiries into tours.

Enrichment AgentCONF 0.95

05Tenant Narratives: Who Belongs in This Building

The Tenant Narrative Specialist generates 3–5 detailed use cases. For a 48,000 SF industrial building with 24-foot clear height: a 3PL operator needing automated racking, a regional food distributor requiring temperature-controlled zones, a contract manufacturer needing heavy power and crane-ready bays, an e-commerce fulfillment center needing high-cube storage, and a building materials distributor needing yard storage. Each narrative is specific enough to guide tenant-rep conversations across the CORFAC network.

Enrichment AgentCONF 0.96

06Continuous Re-Enrichment

The enrichment loop runs continuously across 78 properties. A new comp sale triggers S05 and S02. A new tenant lease triggers S03 and S07. A zoning change triggers S10. On average, 15–20 re-enrichment triggers per day across the portfolio. Re-enrichment is surgical — only affected specialists run.

The operational impact is compound. When the Outreach Agent emails a prospect who expressed interest 3 weeks ago, it references a comp sale that closed yesterday. Every downstream output is as current as the most recent enrichment trigger.

Downstream Impact
The enriched profile is the source of truth for every downstream agent. Targeting reads property type and thesis for prospect calibration. Enrichment quality determines the quality of every other agent’s output. This is why 12 of 18 agents are enrichment specialists.

How does it compare to manual enrichment?+
Manual enrichment requires 2–3 hours of broker time per property. The Enrichment Agent processes the same scope in under 4 minutes with 12 parallel specialists.
What is the difference between a raw listing and an enriched profile?+
A raw listing has ~48 data points: address, sqft, price, zoning. An enriched profile has ~340: investment thesis, 5 tenant narratives, 12 comps, financial models, and buyer-calibrated marketing copy. The listing describes a building. The profile makes a case for it.
