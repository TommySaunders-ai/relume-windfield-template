FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during an initial consultation?

The initial consultation is a 50-minute session where we discuss your concerns, goals, and history. This is a collaborative process to determine the best treatment approach for your unique needs. There is no pressure to commit, and you are welcome to ask questions throughout.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, most major insurance plans are accepted. Please contact the office with your insurance information for verification of benefits and coverage details. Out-of-pocket and sliding scale options are also available.

Is telehealth available?

Yes, telehealth sessions are available for Pennsylvania residents via a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. Telehealth offers the same quality of care as in-person sessions and is ideal for those with scheduling or transportation challenges.

How is clinical hypnotherapy different from stage hypnosis?

Clinical hypnotherapy is a therapeutic technique performed by a licensed clinician in a clinical setting. Unlike stage hypnosis, clinical hypnotherapy is evidence-based, goal-directed, and focused on helping individuals address specific concerns like anxiety, sleep issues, and smoking cessation. You remain fully aware and in control throughout the process.
