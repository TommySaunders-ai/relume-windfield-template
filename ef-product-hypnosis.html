Frequently Asked Questions

Is hypnotherapy safe?

Yes. Clinical hypnotherapy is a well-researched, evidence-based therapeutic technique. You remain conscious and in control throughout every session. It is not mind control and you cannot be made to do anything against your will.

Will I lose control during the session?

No. Hypnosis is a state of focused attention, not unconsciousness. You can open your eyes or stop the recording at any time. Think of it as a deeply relaxed state of concentration, similar to being absorbed in a good book.

How often should I listen to the recordings?

For best results, listen to your chosen recording daily for the first two weeks, then 2-3 times per week for maintenance. The smoking cessation series should be followed in sequence, one session per week.

Can I use these alongside therapy or medication?

Absolutely. These recordings are designed to complement, not replace, professional treatment. Many therapists recommend self-guided hypnosis as a between-session tool. Always consult your provider about integrating new practices.

What format are the recordings in?

All recordings are delivered as high-quality MP3 files that work on any device. You'll receive an instant download link after purchase. Files can be saved to your phone, tablet, or computer.

What if it doesn't work for me?

Every purchase comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you've used the recordings as directed and don't notice a difference, simply reach out for a full refund. No questions asked.
