Approach

Methodology

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

A structured, skills-based approach that teaches clients to manage intense emotions, reduce self-destructive behaviors, and improve relationships through four core skill modules.

Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction

Structured mindfulness training integrating body scan, sitting meditation, and mindful movement to cultivate present-moment awareness and reduce stress reactivity.

Clinical Hypnotherapy

Evidence-based hypnotic techniques utilizing trance states to access subconscious patterns, promote positive behavioral change, and reinforce therapeutic gains.

Positive Outcome Imagery

Guided visualization leveraging the mind-body connection to create new neural pathways, rehearse positive outcomes, and build confidence for real-world situations.
