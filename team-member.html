Nathaniel Rockett

Lead Platform Engineer

Nathaniel leads the platform engineering team at Windfield, where he architects the core infrastructure that powers intelligent property operations. With deep expertise in real-time systems and AI integration, he designed the MCP protocol layer that connects Claude-powered agents to live property data across the entire portfolio.

Before joining Windfield, Nathaniel spent five years building distributed systems at scale, shipping products used by millions of users. He brings a builder's mentality to every problem -- preferring working software over perfect abstractions, and shipping velocity over theoretical elegance.

Outside of engineering, Nathaniel contributes to open-source tooling for agentic workflows and speaks regularly at developer conferences on the intersection of AI agents and real-world operational systems.
