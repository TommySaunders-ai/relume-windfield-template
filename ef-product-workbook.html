What's Included

Six core skill modules covering the full spectrum of distress tolerance techniques.

TIPP Skills

Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, and Progressive relaxation exercises for immediate physiological regulation.

STOP Skill

Stop, Take a step back, Observe, Proceed mindfully. A structured pause technique for high-emotion situations.

Wise Mind ACCEPTS

Activities, Contributing, Comparisons, Emotions, Pushing away, Thoughts, Sensations. Seven distraction strategies for crisis moments.

Radical Acceptance

Exercises for accepting reality as it is without judgment, reducing suffering caused by fighting unchangeable circumstances.

Pros and Cons

Structured decision-making worksheets for evaluating actions during emotional crises, supporting wise choices.

Crisis Survival Quick-Reference

Printable one-page guide with all key skills summarized for fast access during high-stress moments.

How To Use

A simple three-step framework for applying these skills in real time.

1

Identify the Crisis

Recognize when your emotional intensity exceeds your window of tolerance. Use the included rating scale to assess urgency level.

2

Select the Skill

Use the quick-reference guide to match your crisis type with the most effective skill. Each module is organized by situation.

3

Practice & Log

Follow the step-by-step exercise, then log your experience in the built-in tracker to build skill mastery over time.
