Property Description

The parcels along N Broadway Street in Gladstone, MO, provide outstanding visibility and are ideal for development to serve the local community. The first parcel measures approximately 1.26 acres (+/-), while the second spans about 1.36 acres (+/-). Together, they offer a combined area of approximately 2.62 acres (+/-), providing ample space for a wide range of development opportunities. Key highlights include excellent frontage along N Broadway Street, close proximity to a high-traffic commercial corridor, and easy access to major roadways, including nearby highways. These parcels are well-suited for retail, office, or mixed-use development, positioned in a growing community with strong demand for services and amenities.
