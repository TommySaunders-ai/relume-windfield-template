Overview

Meridian Tower is a premier Class A office building located in the heart of Kansas City's thriving downtown corridor. Completed in 2019, this 32-story tower represents the newest addition to the city's skyline and offers best-in-class amenities for today's modern workforce.

The property features 285,000 square feet of leasable space with efficient floor plates averaging 9,200 square feet per floor. Floor-to-ceiling windows on all sides provide panoramic views of the downtown core, the Missouri River, and the surrounding metropolitan area.

Current occupancy stands at 94% with a diversified tenant roster including financial services, technology, and professional services firms. The remaining availability is concentrated on premium upper floors, presenting an opportunity for tenants seeking flagship space or investors looking to capture upside through lease-up.

The building achieved LEED Gold certification at delivery and maintains best-in-class energy performance, positioning it favorably for ESG-focused institutional investors and corporate tenants with sustainability mandates.

Location & Amenities

Neighborhood

Meridian Tower is situated in the heart of Kansas City's Central Business District, surrounded by dining, entertainment, and cultural attractions. The Power & Light District is a two-block walk, providing tenants with immediate access to over 50 restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues. The Kansas City Streetcar stops directly in front of the building, connecting the CBD to the River Market and Crown Center corridors.
