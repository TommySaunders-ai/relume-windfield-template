About Us

Creating Real Value in Commercial Real Estate

Windfield Real Estate LLC / CORFAC International is a full-service commercial real estate firm serving the Kansas City metro area. For over 15 years, we have helped clients buy, sell, lease, develop, and invest in properties, tackling complex challenges and finding opportunities others overlook.

Our Mission

Smarter, Faster, Less Costly

Continually explore new ideas and technology to make the Sales, Leasing, and Development of Commercial Real Estate easier, faster, and less costly.

We believe in a data-driven approach that leverages the latest market insights, technology, and strategic partnerships to deliver measurable results for our clients.

Local Expertise

Deep knowledge of the Kansas City metro market, from North Kansas City industrial corridors to downtown office spaces and suburban retail centers.

Global Reach

As a member of CORFAC International, we connect our clients to a network of commercial real estate professionals in over 70 markets worldwide.

Full-Service Approach

From development consulting to investment services, we provide comprehensive support across the entire commercial real estate lifecycle.
